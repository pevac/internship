Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers
Each website owner must provide identifying information about his or her website through an information depository commonly referred to as "Whois".

ICANN (Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers) oversees the network of websites that make up the Internet. Each website owner must provide identifying information about his or her website through an information depository commonly referred to as "Whois". This information includes the name add address of the website owner as well as that of the web host. You can also setermine the date when a website was first created (published to the web) by looking up Whois data.
